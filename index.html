Trio Kitchen

Authentic Filipino, Indian & Korean Cuisine

explore Trio

We founded Trio Kitchen after many trips to Asia, where we always found ourselves walking the streets of Manila, New Delhi and Seoul in search of the perfect authentic homemade food. The Asian food culture inspires with its flavors and its social food culture. Now we bring our adventure home with us and offer a true experience in an authentic environment. Each goal is to serve authentic food from the Philippines, India and Korea to the heart of Stockholm.
